2. Structure

This section outlines the structure and layout of the website.

  • Header: Contains the website logo on the left and a navigation menu. The header has a green background.
  • Home Page: Features a banner section, followed by a main content area with multiple columns (featured content). Includes a CTA (Call to Action) button.
  • Footer: A dark gray footer containing contact information, social media links, and other footer elements.

3. Color Scheme

This section outlines the color choices for the website.

  • Primary Color (Green): #4CAF50 (used for the header background and CTA buttons)
  • Secondary Color (Dark Gray): #333 (used for footer background and general text)
  • Background Color: #f4f4f4 (light gray for main content background)
  • Text Color: #333 (dark gray for general text)
  • Footer Text Color: #fff (white text on dark gray footer)

4. Typography

The font choices used on the website include:

  • Primary Font: Arial, sans-serif (used for body text and headings)
  • Font Sizes:
    • H1: 2rem
    • H2: 1.6rem
    • Body Text: 1.2rem

5. Design Specifications

Header Design

  • Background color: #4CAF50 (green)
  • Text: White color
  • Font size for title: 2rem

Footer Design

  • Background color: #333 (dark gray)
  • Text: White color
  • Links: White text with hover effect

6. Site Name and Purpose

Site Name: Chamber of Commerce

Reason for Name Selection: This name is directly related to the organization's role of providing services and support to local businesses and community members.

Site Purpose: The website aims to provide relevant information, including local business listings, networking opportunities, event announcements, and important chamber updates for members and visitors.

7. Scenarios

Here are a few common scenarios for users visiting the Chamber of Commerce website:

  • What upcoming business events or networking opportunities are available this month?
  • How can I become a member of the Chamber of Commerce?
  • Who are the board members of the Chamber of Commerce and how can I contact them?
